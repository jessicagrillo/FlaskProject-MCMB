What To Do To A Woman's Body Is A Woman's Choice

​MyChoiceMyBody (MCMB) is an organization founded upon the mission to ensure women continue to retain control over their bodies regardless of the state of the Constitution of the United States. After the recent overturn of Roe v. Wade, numerous women have fallen victim to the restrictions placed upon them by slate legislation. Through the utilization of technology and volunteer resources, our organization presents a software application to be used internally by NPOs to connect individuals of low socioeconomic status who live in states where access to reproductive health services is prohibited with the necessary resources to receive treatment outside of their home state through the utilization of volunteer resources. The value of the application is the ability for NPOs with similar missions to pool resources to maximize the number of individuals assisted. There is no need for women to be victim to the state in which they reside when our country was founded upon the fact that we’re united to ensure the common good of those that inhabit it.
